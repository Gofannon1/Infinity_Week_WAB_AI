•	WAB promotes a framework of Diversity, Equity, Anti-Racism, and Social Justice (I-DEAS).
•	The school celebrates visible and invisible differences, aiming for a culture where every community member feels valued and included.
•	Practices are culturally responsive, aiming to integrate global perspectives while honoring Chinese context.
•	Equity efforts ensure all individuals have access to resources to thrive.
•	The school actively challenges racism and promotes internationally minded hiring and teaching.
•	A symbol of inclusion at WAB is the Chinese character “和” (hé), meaning harmony.
