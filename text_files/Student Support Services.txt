•	Student support at WAB is guided by the belief that every child belongs and should be seen and valued.
•	The support model is collaborative, involving teachers, families, and specialists to create personalized plans.
•	WAB provides learning support, EAL, counseling, and psychological services.
•	Therapies like speech and occupational therapy are integrated when needed.
•	The aim is a safe, inclusive space where all students can succeed both socially and academically.
