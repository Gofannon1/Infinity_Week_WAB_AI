•	All parents and guardians are members of “Parent Link,” WAB’s volunteer parent organization.
•	Parent Link serves as a liaison between families and the school, supporting events and school life.
•	Parents support school activities in arts and sports, host book fairs and social events, and participate in charity efforts.
•	“Parents as Learners” offers workshops on communication, conflict resolution, and study skills.
•	Parents have access to school facilities and libraries, reinforcing WAB’s view of parents as part of the learning community.
