•	Beijing is a vast metropolis of over 22 million people, blending ancient heritage with modern life. Historic sites like the Great Wall and Forbidden City exist alongside contemporary districts like 798 and large shopping malls.
•	A large expat community includes professionals, embassy staff, artists, and students, supporting many international clubs, supermarkets, and services.
•	Beijing has four seasons: spring and fall are mild, winters are cold and dry (often using air purifiers), and summers are hot and humid.
•	Imported and local food is widely available. International stores carry Western products, and the restaurant scene includes both local Chinese food and global cuisines.
•	Despite its size, Beijing offers many green spaces and is close to mountains and winter resorts for outdoor activities.
•	The city is a major travel hub for exploring China and Asia, with extensive air and high-speed rail connections.
